FAQ

Disneyland Paris transfer: frequently asked questions

How much is a private transfer to Disneyland Paris?

The private transfer to Disneyland Paris is a fixed price, given and confirmed before booking via our online quote. It can include round-trip transport and, optionally, park tickets.

How long is the Paris to Disneyland ride?

Usually 40 to 50 minutes between Paris and Disneyland Paris (Marne-la-Vallee). Direct from CDG or Orly too.

Does the package include park tickets?

Yes, it can include your Disneyland Park tickets, or the 2-Park option (Disneyland + Walt Disney Studios), plus the private round-trip transport.

Which vehicle for a family with children and luggage?

The Mercedes V-Class van (up to 7-8 passengers) is ideal for families, groups, strollers and luggage. Child seats available on request.

Will the chauffeur pick me up at the end of the day?

Yes. You choose the return time: your chauffeur picks you up at Disneyland Paris and drives you back.
