What to see in Paris in 3 days: the first-timer's guide

First time in Paris with only 3 days? Here is a ready-made day-by-day itinerary with the must-sees and the activities not to miss — so you see it all without rushing.

Day 1 — The icons of Paris

  • 1
    Eiffel Tower & Trocadéro. The most iconic view: go up to the top, or take the perfect photo from Trocadéro.
  • 2
    Champs-Élysées & Arc de Triomphe. The world's most beautiful avenue; climb the Arc for the view over the 12 avenues.
  • 3
    Seine river cruise (evening). Paris lit up from the river — a magical way to end the day.

Day 2 — Art, history and Montmartre

  • 1
    The Louvre. The Mona Lisa and endless masterpieces — book a time slot in advance.
  • 2
    Notre-Dame & Île de la Cité. The historic heart of Paris, with Sainte-Chapelle nearby.
  • 3
    Latin Quarter. Cobbled streets, bookshops and cafés — the Left Bank atmosphere.
  • 4
    Montmartre & Sacré-Cœur (sunset). The artists' village and the finest panorama over Paris.

Day 3 — Pick a big experience

  • A
    Palace of Versailles (day trip). The palace, the Hall of Mirrors and Le Nôtre's gardens.
  • B
    Disneyland Paris (with family). A day of magic in Marne-la-Vallée.
  • C
    Le Marais + shopping + cabaret night. To stay in Paris: charming districts, boutiques and the Moulin Rouge in the evening.
