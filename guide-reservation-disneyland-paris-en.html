Booking Disneyland Paris with a private chauffeur: how it works

Want to enjoy Disneyland Paris with zero logistics? Here is, step by step, how your day with a private chauffeur works, the difference between the two parks, the must-see attractions and shows, and the big 2026 new experiences.

How your booking works

Everything is designed so you have nothing to organise: you book, we handle the rest.

  • ✓
    We contact you 24h before. We call or message you to confirm the timing, the pick-up address and your preferences.
  • ✓
    Named tickets emailed 24h before. Your tickets are issued in your name and reach you the day before: you walk straight in, no queue at the counters.
  • ✓
    Unlimited use on the day. You can enter and leave the parks as often as you like throughout your reserved day.
  • ✓
    In direct contact with your chauffeur. You agree together on the drop-off and pick-up times: the chauffeur drops you off and comes back for you.
  • ✓
    Private vehicle. The vehicle is reserved just for you and your party, both ways, from Paris, the airport or your hotel.

One or two parks? The difference

Disneyland Paris is two neighbouring parks, a few minutes' walk apart, with very different atmospheres.

Because our tickets allow unlimited re-entry, you can move between the parks during the day if your ticket covers both.

Attractions & entertainment

At Disneyland Park: Big Thunder Mountain, Pirates of the Caribbean, Phantom Manor, Peter Pan's Flight, "it's a small world", Star Wars Hyperspace Mountain, plus the parades on Main Street.

At Disney Adventure World: Frozen Ever After (World of Frozen), Rapunzel (Tangled Spin), Avengers Campus (Spider-Man, Iron Man), Ratatouille, Crush's Coaster and the Tower of Terror.

Light shows & nighttime spectaculars

At Disneyland Park, the nighttime show lights up Sleeping Beauty Castle: video mapping, lasers, water jets and fireworks — a magical finale not to be missed.

At Disney Adventure World, the brand-new Disney Cascade of Lights takes place on the great Adventure Bay lake: giant water screens, projections, fireworks and a choreography of aquatic and aerial drones.

The parks' restaurants

For a gourmet break: Auberge de Cendrillon (character dining in Fantasyland), Walt's – An American Restaurant on Main Street, Blue Lagoon in the heart of Pirates of the Caribbean, or Bistrot Chez Rémy (Ratatouille world) at Disney Adventure World — which also gained 15 new dining locations with its transformation.

2026 new experiences

On 29 March 2026, the second park became Disney Adventure World, with a brand-new avenue, Adventure Way, and a large central lake, Adventure Bay.

  • ★
    World of Frozen. The Kingdom of Arendelle recreated at life size, the family attraction Frozen Ever After, a daytime show with a life-size Olaf and a royal encounter with Anna and Elsa.
  • ★
    Rapunzel – Tangled Spin. A new attraction inspired by the film Tangled, along Adventure Way.
  • ★
    Disney Cascade of Lights. The new nighttime spectacular on Adventure Bay.
  • ★
    Coming next. A first attraction inspired by The Lion King and a flying carousel themed to Up.
