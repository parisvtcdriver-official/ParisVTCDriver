CDG → Disneyland Paris: taxi, RER or VTC?

Landing at Paris-Charles de Gaulle airport and heading to Disneyland Paris with your luggage and children? Here are the 3 options compared, so you can pick the easiest one.

The 3 ways to reach Disneyland from CDG

Disneyland Paris is in Marne-la-Vallée, about 40 km from CDG airport. Three options: the RER/train, a taxi, or a private VTC.

OptionDurationComfort
RER B + RER A (with change)~1h15 to 1h30Cheap but tiring with luggage and kids, several changes
Taxi~45 minDirect but variable price, no guaranteed flight tracking
Private VTC~45 minDoor-to-door, fixed price, meet & greet, child seat on request

  • ✓
